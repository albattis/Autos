Meltingly Delicious Handmade Fudge

Our fudge range has grown along with the Gardiners brand to offer a truly meltingly delicious fudge confectionery that is sold across the world. Gardiners of Scotland is a long established family run business with a real passion for fudge. Since we were founded all those years ago, every single batch of our fudge has been handmade to a traditional Scottish recipe.

For decades, we have made sweet confectionery using the best ingredients in our very own factory based in the village of Lesmahagow. From the best sugar and milk products, natural spices, oils, extracts and spirits, we create our fudge confectionery that truly is meltingly delicious. We have partnered with some of the best whisky brands both in Scotland and across the pond to offer whisky flavoured fudge that has become a firm favourite with so many whisky fans.
